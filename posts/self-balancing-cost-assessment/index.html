Self-Balancing Cost Assessment

An Engineer

2020-08-02 01:59

Comments

Source

Purpose

As is often the case for many of my DIY projects, one the first steps I take is a cost assessment and/or product search. Is there already something that does what I want? And how much does it cost? What are the advantages and disadvantages to doing it myself? I'll try to answer all those questions in a later post, but for now I'll look at cost.
